# Book Dragon (a Snake Game)

A browser-based snake game where you are a dragon hoarding books, evading the TBR police, and growing your library one title at a time.

**[Play it here →](https://agneathad.github.io/book-dragon-snake-game/)**



## How to play

- Use arrow keys, WASD, the on-screen d-pad, or swipe to move
- Collect 📙 books to grow your hoard
- Avoid 👮 the TBR police, they will confiscate everything
- You have 3 lives; lose them all and it's game over

## Difficulty levels

| Level | Speed | TBR police |
|---|---|---|
| 🥚 Hatchling | Slow | Spawn every 5 books |
| 🐉 Dragon | Medium | Spawn every 3 books |
| 💀 Ancient Evil | Fast | Spawn every 2 books |

## Power-ups

| Item | Effect |
|---|---|
| ☕ Coffee | Tops up a life (max 3) |
| 🔖 Bookmark | Slows speed for 5 seconds — dragon turns blue |
| 👓 Reading glasses | Soft walls, vanquish police on contact, dragon turns gold — 8 seconds |

## Features

- 3 lives - lose one and keep playing without interruption
- Speed increases gradually as your library grows
- Best hoard saved to browser storage and persists between sessions
- Fully playable on desktop and mobile

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript — single file, no dependencies
